What does Gabrova mean?

2206 Gabrova is an asteroid located in the Asteroid Belt, discovered on April 1, 1976 by Russian astronomer Nikolai Chernykh at the Crimean Astrophysical Observatory.

Since it was discovered on April Fool's Day, the asteroid is named after the Bulgarian city of Gabrovo, known for its jokes and as the capital of humor and satire.
