Saturn

Saturn is the second largest of the eight planets in the Solar System. In Roman mythology, Saturn is the god of time and wealth. Saturn is known for it's prominent rings. It is visibly flattened at the poles and features a hexagonal cloud at the north pole. 83 known satellites exist, 53 of them are named. The biggest of those moons are Titan, which is bigger than Mercury, Rhea, Iapetus, Dione, Tethys, Enceladus and Mimas.
