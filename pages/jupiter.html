Jupiter

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System and a gas giant. It bears the name of the king of gods and god of the sky in Roman mythology. The most recognisable feature is the storm, known as Great Red Spot. It has a faint ring and at least 80 moons, the four main ones being Ganymede, Callisto, Io and Europe. Ganymede is also the biggest satellite in the Solar System, bigger than the planet Mercury.
